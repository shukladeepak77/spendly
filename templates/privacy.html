Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your financial records.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers to host and operate the service, and these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on our site. We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries promptly.
